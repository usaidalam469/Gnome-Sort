-------------------->GNOME SORT<--------------------
It is the sorting algorithm to sort series of numbers which is similar to insertion sort in that it works with one item at a time but gets the item to the proper place by a series of swaps, similar to a bubble sort. 

How to run?
Enter the series of numbers and select whether to sort this series in ascending or descending order and then you'll get the required result. 

Works for?
- Works for every positive or negative integer values.
- Avoid entering characters or decimal values.

Platforms:
-Visual Studio
